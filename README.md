Project Title

E-Commerce Product Detail Page E-Commerce Product Detail Page Project Type

Business

Problem the System Addresses

Many online shoppers find it difficult to understand products when information is not clearly presented. This system solves that problem by displaying all important product details (image, price, description, and options) in a clear and organized way, helping users make better purchasing decisions. High-Quality Product Images: Display clear images and allow users to view the product from different angles. Detailed Product Description: Provide enough information about the product so customers understand its features and benefits. Product Comparison Information: Show important details like price, rating, and specifications to help customers compare products. User-Friendly Interface: Make the page simple and easy to navigate so customers can quickly find what they need.

Target Audience

Online shoppers looking for products to buy

Customers comparing products before purchasing

Returning customers who already shop online

New visitors browsing products on an online store
